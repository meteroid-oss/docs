title: Integrations
---


The **Integrations** section in Meteroid allows you to connect with various external systems, including **payment providers, CRM platforms, and accounting systems**. These integrations help streamline billing, payment processing, customer management, and financial reconciliation. To set up an integration, navigate to **Settings > Integrations**, select the system you want to connect, and follow the setup instructions.
